Social & Community Participation

Fostering inclusivity and engagement for vibrant community participation and growth

Unlock the possibilities of your NDIS Plan with Social and Community Participation funding, nestled within the realms of CORE and CAPACITY BUILDING sections. In the Core Supports arena, discover it as "Assistance with Social and Community Participation" (Support Category 1.04).

What is Social and Community Participation?

This funding is your key to community engagement, honing social skills, and elevating your well-being through meaningful connections and active involvement in a tapestry of social and recreational pursuits

The Big Oxmox advised her not to do so, because there were thousands of bad Commas, wild Question Marks and devious Semikoli, but the Little Blind Text didn’t listen. She packed her seven versalia, put her initial into the belt and made herself on the way.

Delving into the fabric of Social and Community Participation, it's a tailored support category under Australia's National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS). Its purpose? To guide NDIS participants in navigating their communities, immersing in social activities, and refining crucial social skills.

Empowering Lives Through NDIS: Building Community Connections and Social Flourish

The ultimate goal is an enriched life - one of independence, quality, and well-being, achieved by cultivating meaningful connections and an active presence in community life.

Bearing two wings, the NDIS encompasses CORE supports and CAPACITY BUILDING supports in a participant's plan. "Assistance with Social and Community Participation" falls under Support Category 4, nestled within Core Supports, designed to weave participants into the community fabric.

This support spans various activities and services promoting social interaction, community engagement, and skill development. Face-to-face services take the spotlight in this funding category.

Examples? Think personal development courses, social group memberships, outings, holidays, library visits, movies, concerts, sporting clubs, and community events. The aim? Enhanced social skills, meaningful connections, and a vibrant participation in community life.

Diverse Opportunities for Social and Community Engagement

  • Attending personal development courses
  • Joining social groups
  • Going on outings or holidays
  • Participating in holiday camps
  • Visiting local libraries
  • Attending movies or concerts
  • Joining sporting clubs
  • Attending community events

But wait, there's more! Enter "Increased Social and Community Participation," residing in Support Category 9 under Capacity Building Supports. This support ignites the flame of social and community engagement skills, paving the way for active involvement in community activities and social interactions.

Our Holistic Approach to Community and Social Support

Our seasoned staff takes pride in more than just support — we're dedicated to fostering social skills development. With a focus on building confidence and effective communication in various social settings, we create an environment where individuals can thrive.

We recognize the importance of collaboration. That's why we actively engage with families and caregivers in our efforts. Through collaborative initiatives, we aim to build strong support networks, ensuring that participants not only receive support but also contribute meaningfully to their communities.
